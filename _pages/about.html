Core Values

Clarity

The better I get at programming, the more I appreciate the value of creating well-structured, readable code. This appreciation has extended to the entire creative process. How do we define the goal and scope of a problem? How do we make products that are responsive to and the meet the needs of users?

Communication

Professionally, I often have to share technical insights with folks who may not have a strong understanding of that domain. I used to teach English in China, and I often reflect on the interconnectedness of these two experiences. How do we share knowledge and information in ways that are accessible to our audience? In ways that keep them engaged?

Empathy

Empathy is challenging. Sometimes it's hard to step back and recognize the needs of others - particularly when they differ so greatly from our own. How do we set aside our beliefs and experiences to receive the perspectives of others? How can we learn to listen effectively, and meet people where they are, not where we want them to be?

Fun

As a programmer and problem-solver, I like puzzles and don't mind banging my head against the wall! But that's not everyone's cup of tea. On the other hand, CPA's may enjoy working with taxes, but that drives me crazy. How do we keep people engaged in what's important, even when it's, well...boring? How can we make work and learning fun?
